Add your wallets
Setup Guide Wallets
Add your wallet by ENS name, or Ethereum EVM address. The name is your choice, for display purposes only. The address is never stored on a centralized server. It stays in your browser’s localStorage only, and gets sent to the data providers (e.g., Alchemy, Etherscan) when we request any blockchain data for the first time.

Etherscan keys
Setup Guide Keys Etherscan
This guide show you how to find/obtain your Etherscan API private keys, and provide it securely. Funds are SAFU. The concept Etherscan refers generally to the various chain explorer APIs across EVM L1s as well as L2s. Etherscan refers to Ethereum Mainnet. PolygonScan refers to Polygon POS chain. Optimistic-Etherscan refers to Optimism. Arbiscan refers to Arbitrum One.
